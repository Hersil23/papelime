Sustainability

100% recyclable, bottle and cap.

Promoting environmental preservation and fostering recycling campaigns is our commitment — it's written in our vision. The packaging is part of the product.

01

Fully recyclable

Every Papelime bottle and cap is 100% recyclable. No mixed-material laminates, no opaque foil seals, no shrink sleeves to peel off.

02

Reusable by design

An ecological replacement for old water bottles — easy to carry in bags, backpacks, and offices. Rinse, refill, take it with you.

03

Local supply chain

Bottled in Northern Virginia, sold in Northern Virginia. Shorter routes, less freight, more dollars staying in the neighborhood.
